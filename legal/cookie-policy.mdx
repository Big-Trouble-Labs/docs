## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They allow the Service to recognise your device, maintain your session, and remember your preferences.

## How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent the Service from working correctly, as authentication depends on them.
